About this game

Omok (오목) is Gomoku — two players alternate placing black and white stones on a 15×15 grid. The first to align five stones horizontally, vertically, or diagonally wins. Black always plays first and opens at the center. The AI picks its moves from candidate squares near existing stones. Source on GitHub.
